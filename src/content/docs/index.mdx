## What is LLMO?

**LLMO (Large Language Model Optimization)** is the practice of optimizing web content so that AI systems — including ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity — can accurately discover, understand, and cite it.

LLMO includes approaches such as AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) and GEO (Generative Engine Optimization).

## The LLMO Framework

The LLMO Framework defines five core components for AI discoverability:

1. **Knowledge Clarity** — Is your content clear enough for AI to understand?
2. **Structural Formatting** — Is your content structured for machine consumption?
3. **Retrieval Signals** — Can AI systems find your content?
4. **Authority Signals** — Does your content demonstrate expertise and trust?
5. **Citation Signals** — Does your content provide references AI can verify?
