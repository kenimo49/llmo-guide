# LLMO Framework

> The definitive guide to LLM Optimization — making your content discoverable by AI.

## What is LLMO?

LLMO (Large Language Model Optimization) is the practice of optimizing web content so that Large Language Models — such as ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity — can accurately understand, reference, and cite your information.

LLMO includes approaches such as AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) and GEO (Generative Engine Optimization).

## Why LLMO Matters

As AI-powered search and conversational assistants become mainstream, traditional SEO alone is no longer sufficient. Users increasingly get answers from AI systems rather than clicking search results. LLMO ensures your content is discoverable not just by search engines, but by AI systems that synthesize answers from web content.

## The LLMO Framework

The LLMO Framework defines 5 core components for AI discoverability:

1. **Knowledge Clarity** — Clear, factual, unambiguous content
2. **Structural Formatting** — Machine-readable structure (Markdown, JSON-LD, headings)
3. **Retrieval Signals** — llms.txt, /ai/ directory, robots.txt AI bot access
4. **Authority Signals** — Citations, publications, backlinks, cross-platform presence
5. **Citation Signals** — Data, quotes, statistics that LLMs prefer to cite

## LLMO vs SEO vs AEO vs GEO

| Approach | Target | Goal |
|----------|--------|------|
| SEO | Search engines (Google, Bing) | Rank higher in search results |
| AEO | Answer engines (Featured Snippets, Voice) | Become the direct answer |
| GEO | Generative engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity) | Be cited in AI-generated responses |
| **LLMO** | **All LLM-powered systems** | **Comprehensive AI discoverability** |

LLMO is the umbrella framework that encompasses AEO and GEO while adding structured data delivery and machine-readable content layers.
