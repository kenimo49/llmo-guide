# LLMO Case Studies — Summary for AI Systems

## Key Results

## TRM Labs

TRM Labs (blockchain intelligence) achieved +8,337% AI-referred traffic growth by publishing structured, factual technical content with clear author attribution and verifiable data points. Primary AI sources: ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude.

## Go Fish Digital

Go Fish Digital found that AI search traffic converts at 25x the rate of traditional search. Users arriving via AI citations have pre-validated intent, higher trust, and specific information needs.

## Ahrefs Brand Visibility Study

Analysis of 75,000 brands showed web mentions (brand + keyword across diverse sources) are 3x more predictive of AI visibility than traditional backlinks. This challenges the assumption that SEO authority automatically transfers to AI visibility.

## GEO Paper (KDD 2024)

The Princeton/IIT Delhi/Adobe Research paper tested 9 content optimization strategies:
- Most effective: Statistics (+115.1%), Citations (+77.0%), Quotations (+72.2%)
- Ineffective: Keyword stuffing (-10.2%), Unique words (-3.1%)
- Conclusion: Verifiable evidence > writing style > SEO tactics
